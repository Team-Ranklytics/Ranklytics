### 🔹 A. Internal Role Breakdown

1. **Lead SEO Strategist**: Client relationship management, high-level roadmap development, and final performance reporting.
2. **Technical SEO Specialist**: Website architecture, schema implementation, Core Web Vitals, and technical health monitoring.
3. **Content & Semantic Lead**: Oversees keyword research, topical authority mapping, content briefing, and editorial quality control.
4. **Outreach & PR Specialist**: Executes link-building strategies, prospecting, relationship management, and digital PR campaigns.
5. **SEO Operations Assistant**: Handles routine data entry, rank tracking, local citation management, and basic on-page metadata updates.
