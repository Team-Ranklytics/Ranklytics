## Acquisition Blueprint

### 🔹 A. Inbound & Authority Building
*   **Owned Media:** Deploy high-conversion landing pages and a technical SEO blog.
*   **Content Syndication:** Publish data-driven case studies on LinkedIn, Medium, and industry-specific forums to establish expertise.
*   **Local Search Dominance:**
    *   **GBP Management:** Weekly updates, keyword-rich descriptions, and proactive review acquisition.
    *   **NAP Consistency:** Audit and synchronize Name, Address, and Phone data across all local directories.
    *   **Geo-Targeted SEO:** Rank for high-intent queries like *"SEO agency in Chennai"* or *"SEO consultant [Location]"*.

### 🔹 B. Proactive Outreach & Partnerships
*   **Cold Email (The Value-First Model):**
    *   *Framework:* Identify specific technical audit fail → Proposed solution → Case study link → Low-friction CTA (15-min call).
*   **Social Selling (LinkedIn):**
    *   *Workflow:* Engage with prospect content → Share relevant insights → DM follow-up with a personalized video audit.
*   **Strategic Alliances:**
    *   Partner with Web Development and Branding agencies to create a reciprocal referral loop.
*   **Referral Program:**
    *   Formalize incentives (e.g., 10% commission or service credits) for existing clients who provide successful introductions.
