## Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

### 🔹 A. Client Onboarding SOP
* **Discovery Phase:** Detailed questionnaire covering business goals, target audience, and USPs.
* **Access Management:** Secure handover of Google Search Console, GA4, CMS, and Tag Manager.
* **Competitor Analysis:** Identification of top 3-5 organic competitors and keyword gap analysis.

### 🔹 B. Monthly SEO Execution Cycle
* **Week 1: Audit & Strategy:** Technical health check and keyword research for new opportunities.
* **Week 2: On-Page & Technical:** Optimization of meta tags, schema markup, and site speed.
* **Week 3: Content & Authority:** Production of SEO-optimized content and backlink outreach.
* **Week 4: Reporting & Review:** Data analysis, KPI tracking, and strategy alignment for the next month.

### 🔹 C. Quality Control Checklist
* **Technical QC:** Verification of mobile responsiveness, site speed, and schema markup.
* **Content QC:** Checks for keyword density, internal linking, and plagiarism.
* **Final Review:** Standardized verification of all deliverables against the project scope.

### 🔹 D. Reporting Template
* **Performance Metrics:** Monthly dashboards highlighting Organic Traffic, CTR, and Conversions.
* **Keyword Tracking:** Progress reports on primary and secondary keyword rankings.
* **Actionable Insights:** Summary of wins, blockers, and strategic recommendations for the next cycle.
