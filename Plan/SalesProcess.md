## Sales Process & Client Lifecycle

1.  **Lead Qualification & Discovery Call**
    *   **Goal:** Determine fit using BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline).
    *   **Action:** Identify current pain points, organic traffic plateaus, and revenue goals.

2.  **Strategic Audit & Tailored Proposal**
    *   **Goal:** Demonstrate immediate value and technical expertise.
    *   **Action:** Perform a "mini-audit" (technical health, keyword gaps). Present a tiered proposal focusing on ROI rather than just tasks.

3.  **Closing: Contract & Onboarding**
    *   **Goal:** Legal protection and financial commitment.
    *   **Action:** Sign Master Service Agreement (MSA) and Statement of Work (SOW). Collect 100% of the first month or a 50% deposit before work begins.

4.  **The "SEO Reality" Phase (Months 1–3)**
    *   **Goal:** Set expectations and build the foundation.
    *   **Action:** Focus on technical debt, site speed, and "low-hanging fruit" content. Educate the client that SEO is a compounding asset, not an instant switch.

5.  **Execution, Reporting & Transparency**
    *   **Goal:** Maintain trust through visible progress.
    *   **Action:** Monthly deliverables (backlinks, content pieces). Send a monthly video walkthrough (Loom) of the KPI dashboard (Looker Studio/GA4).

6.  **Quarterly Strategy Review (QSR)**
    *   **Goal:** High-level alignment and contract renewal.
    *   **Action:** Review macro-trends, competitor shifts, and pivot the strategy for the next 90 days.

**Essential Sales Assets:**

*   **Proposal Template:** Value-based pricing tiers with clear "What's Included"

*   **Service Agreement:** Clauses for scope creep, termination notice (30 days), and IP ownership.

*   **Onboarding Questionnaire:** To gather access (GA4, GSC, CMS) and brand guidelines efficiently.

*   **Automated Invoicing:** Recurring billing setup (Razorpay) to avoid chasing payments.
